What does “breathing down her neck” mean here?
It is pressure that is close and constant — the deadline is not far off any more, it is right behind her. Same shape as running out of time, but it puts a person or a date at your shoulder.

A teacher who has the whole video

The chat has the video and its subtitles in context, so “what did she mean by that?” is already enough to go on. In a hurry, click a word right in the subtitles — it's a shortcut for the same question.

Lex does not replace a good teacher, and it would be dishonest to say otherwise. A person hears the thing you didn't say. They notice you've been quietly avoiding a whole tense for a month. They push you on a day you'd rather not be pushed. Lex won't do any of that. What it will do is be there for all the hours in between — on the video you actually wanted to watch, with nobody else around to ask. If you already have a tutor, keep them.
