### What you are buying, and what it costs

Lex is a Chrome extension. It is an English teacher that works on a YouTube video you choose. It gives you its own readable subtitles for that video and a chat with the teacher, by text or by voice. Clicking a word is a shortcut for asking the teacher about it. Lex works only on YouTube.

**How payment works.** Installing the extension and creating an account are free. To take lessons you top up a prepaid balance in US dollars, and the balance is spent as you use the service. There is no subscription, no auto-renewal and no recurring charge. Nothing is ever taken from your card automatically. $10 is the usual first top-up.

**How the price is calculated.** Lex has no flat fee and no fixed price per lesson: your balance is drawn down in proportion to how much you actually use the teacher, in text and in voice. The extension shows your current balance and the cost of each interaction as it happens, so you can see what you are spending before and after every use. The current rates are published at https://lex-me.club/#install. No discounts are applied at present.

**Paid actions inside the extension.** Once your balance is topped up, it is what pays for lessons, voice sessions and pronunciation checks. All of them are paid from your balance and never from your card, the cost of each is shown to you, and no amount leaves your balance without an action you take yourself — no setting allows an automatic purchase.

**Your balance is an advance payment.** The money you top up is an advance against lessons you have not yet taken. It is not a deposit or a security, and no amount is ever blocked on your card. The balance does not expire.

**Taxes and the total you pay.** Top-up amounts are shown in US dollars. If you withdraw after lessons have started with your consent, we keep an amount proportionate to what you actually used and return the rest. Your confirmation is repeated in the payment confirmation email.

**Our own refund promise, wider than the law.** Even after the 14 days, your **unspent** balance stays refundable for as long as your account exists. Write to nikolaev.gd@gmail.com.
