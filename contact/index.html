What you are buying, and what it costs

Lex is a Chrome extension. It is an English teacher that works on a YouTube video you choose. It gives you its own readable subtitles for that video and a chat with the teacher, by text or by voice. Clicking a word is a shortcut for asking the teacher about it. Lex works only on YouTube.

How payment works. Installing the extension and creating an account are free. To take lessons you top up a prepaid balance in US dollars, and the balance is spent as you use the service. There is no subscription, no auto-renewal and no recurring charge. Nothing is ever taken from your card automatically. $10 is the usual first top-up.

How the price is calculated. Lex has no flat fee and no fixed price per lesson: your balance is drawn down in proportion to how much you actually use the teacher, in text and in voice. The extension shows your current balance and the cost of each interaction as it happens, so you can see what you are spending before and after every use. The current rates are published at https://lex-me.club/#install. No discounts are applied at present.

Paid actions inside the extension. Once your balance is topped up, it is what pays for lessons, voice sessions and pronunciation checks. All of them are paid from your balance and never from your card, the cost of each is shown to you, and no amount leaves your balance without an action you take yourself — no setting allows an automatic purchase.

Your balance is an advance payment. The money you top up is an advance against lessons you have not yet taken. It is not a deposit or a security, and no amount is ever blocked on your card. The balance does not expire.

Taxes and the total you pay. Top-up amounts are shown in US dollars. Depending on where you live and which payment channel is used, the payment provider may add local consumption tax at checkout, and if your card is not issued in USD your bank applies its own conversion rate. The full amount you will be charged and the amount that will be credited to your balance are both shown on the payment page before you confirm. We add no charge of our own on top of the top-up. As for our own tax status: A.I. Ghenadi Nicolaev is not registered as a VAT payer in the Republic of Moldova. Turnover is below the registration threshold set by art. 112 of the Fiscal Code, so no VAT is charged and prices carry no VAT.

Delivery. Delivery is electronic and immediate: the balance appears in your account as soon as the payment is confirmed. There are no delivery, postal, packaging or transport costs, because there is nothing to ship. We apply no geographic restriction — the balance is credited wherever you are. Any restriction on your country or your card comes from the payment provider and is shown to you before you pay.

Payment terms. Payment is made in full before the balance is credited. Accepted means of payment: payment card, on the payment provider's secure page. Card details are entered on the payment provider's own secure page — they never reach us and we never store them. 3-D Secure authentication applies. If a refund is due, it goes back by the same method the payment was made.

How long the offer and the rates hold. The top-up amounts and usage rates shown on this site and in the extension are valid for Prices are those shown on this site at the moment you place an order, and stay valid until we change them here. from the "last updated" date at the bottom of this page. If we change the rates at which an existing balance is drawn down, we tell you by email before the change takes effect, and you may ask for your unspent balance back instead.

How long the contract lasts, and how to end it. The contract has no fixed term and no minimum duration. Your only obligation is the top-up you chose to make; nothing further is ever owed. You can stop using Lex at any moment. To close your account, write to nikolaev.gd@gmail.com — we return your unspent balance and delete your data as described in the Privacy Policy, with the exception noted there.

System requirements. Google Chrome (the extension is built on Manifest V3), a Lex account, and a positive balance for lessons.

Your right to withdraw, and refunds

The legal right to withdraw (drept de revocare). Lex is digital content supplied without a tangible medium. Under art. 1059 and art. 1060 alin. (1) lit. f) of the Civil Code of the Republic of Moldova you have 14 calendar days from the day your top-up is credited to withdraw from the purchase. You do not have to give a reason and you owe no penalty. Send us an unequivocal statement at nikolaev.gd@gmail.com; you may also use the standard withdrawal form (formularul standard de revocare, Anexa nr. 6 la Legea nr. 1125/2002), which is reproduced in our Terms and Conditions. We reimburse you within 14 calendar days of receiving your notice, by the same means of payment you used, at no cost to you.

When part of that right is lost. If you want lessons to start before the 14 days are up, we ask you at checkout for your express consent to start immediately and for your separate confirmation that, for the part already performed, you thereby lose the right to withdraw (art. 1065 alin. (1) lit. m)). We never infer that consent from a pre-ticked box. If you withdraw after lessons have started with your consent, we keep an amount proportionate to what you actually used and return the rest. Your confirmation is repeated in the payment confirmation email.

Our own refund promise, wider than the law. Even after the 14 days, your unspent balance stays refundable for as long as your account exists. Write to nikolaev.gd@gmail.com. Balance you have already spent on lessons is not refundable. We process refund requests within 14 days of receiving them and charge no fee; how long the money then takes to reach you depends on your bank.
